Mission

Enable agricultural and industrial outposts in space. Help set the foundations for a Type III civilization. Our existing mission is to help feed the world quality and nutritious foods by going to space. We must and we will create beautiful & healthy surroundings on and off of earth. For ourselves and the future generations.

Below: A semi-autonomous manufacturing site that can make copies of itself. As well, enable inventions of our choosing. This is our invention and we have simple set of simulations here. In the future, we'd love to work with Mitsubishi electric and other robotic companies.


Our submission to the Governmet of Canada's 'Deep Space Food Challenge'. We scaled down our designs in order to meet the constraints outlined in the challenge. Unfortunately we were not chosen as one of the teams that will proceed to the next level. Our dream is to have hundreds of thousands or millions of such facilities in non-terrestrial orbits (Meaning, not Earth's orbit). This is one design that we will continue iterating on. Because this is the design that will help solve hunger and malnutrition. Quality, nutritious foods that are free of carcinogenics and harmful chemicals.



The '3 stage' AMU (semi-autonomous manufacturing unit). Includes, the main manufacturing site (MMS) (can replicate itself and re-organize itself in order to make different inventions), fuel tanks and a transporter for moving goods and services. Produce will be washed, dried and then flash-frozen without the use of chemicals. Multiple transporters will dock into a singular body (MMS). The re-entry problem is being solved by a lot of other companies. The overall cycle looks something like this: Food is grown in semi-autonomous AMUS --> Transporters take the produce and do a hand-off with a mechanism in Earth's orbit --> Re-entry and landing at a commercial space port --> Autonomous trucks coupled with robotics. Overall, making heavy use of robotics and automation is pivotal towards feeding all humans, pets and potentially other animals.
