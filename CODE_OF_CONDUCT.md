# Code of Conduct

Awakened Imagination is dedicated to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive community. We expect all contributors and participants to adhere to this code of conduct at all times.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, or religion (or lack thereof).

Expected behavior includes:

- Being kind, courteous, and respectful
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others
- Collaborating openly and transparently

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behaviors include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments, verbal abuse, or threats
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behavior

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub repositories, discussions, and any other communication channels. It also applies to public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Enforcement

We take violations of this code of conduct seriously. Reports will be reviewed by the project maintainers and addressed appropriately.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it immediately by contacting the project leads at [insert contact email] or through GitHub's reporting tools.

Your report will be handled with discretion and seriousness.
