Plan for Global Prosperity

"Healing the Biosphere by Moving Industry Off-World"

  • Step 1: Identify the most ecologically destructive heavy industries on Earth.
  • Step 2: Rapidly prototype off-world equivalents using our 3-stage AMU technology.
  • Step 3: Establish the high-cadence orbital-to-terrestrial delivery loop for refined materials.
  • Step 4: Coordinated decommissioning of Earth-based heavy manufacturing plants.
  • Step 5: Intensive biosphere restoration through re-wilding and atmospheric remediation.
