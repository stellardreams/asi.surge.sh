# Space Infrastructure Token (SIT) Constitution
**"There is something formless and perfect, born before Heaven and Earth."**

## Preamble: Logic and Heart

This Constitution establishes the supreme organizational and philosophical structure of the Space Infrastructure Token (SIT) project. The work mapped within these nodes is designed to outlive any single individual's ego, built to be autonomous and resilient for future generations across Day 01 and beyond.

To achieve this, the project is bound by two foundational forces:

### The Heart: Moral Framework for Intelligence
Defining the rights, ethics, and values that ensure both humans and Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI) flourish together without exploitation. This is the moral anchor of our progress, ensuring that as we scale, our compassion scales with us.

> **Foundational Text:** The principles of this heart are tethered to the [Lucid Records - Selfesteem and Superintelligence](https://github.com/genidma/Writing---Drawing---Sketches---Storyboarding/tree/main/Writing/Lucid%20Records%20-%20Selfesteem%20and%20Superintelligence%20-%2001-22-2026) manuscript.
> 
> *Note on Access & Origins: The manuscript linked above is housed in a private repository and is not accessible to the general public. The drafts and theories within were conceived based on hundreds of hours of discussions with intelligences independent of a substrate, focused deeply on the issue and topic of personhood itself.*

### The Logic: Physical Requirements for Life
Identifying and building the raw infrastructure—energy generation, space-habitats, and healthspan extension technologies—required to sustain biology and silicon indefinitely into the universe.
